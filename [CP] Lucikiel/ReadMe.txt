Arknir's Guide to Lucikiel - Version 1.9 (Updated for SDV 1.5)

Stardew Valley Expanded update!
Fixed the schedules and events to match all the new maps.

Update 1.6 - You can now change which version of the portraits you want for Lucikiel in the config.json (*Upon starting game with SMAPI, a config.json will be created.)
- Set "AnimePortraits" as "true" for anime portraits (by Kono437816)
- Set "AnimePortraits" as "false" for the original vanilla portraits (by Arknir)
*The PyTK mod must be installed to view the anime portraits properly. (Located in the 'Requirements' in the mod description page of Lucikiel)

Update 1.7 - Lucikiel's beach costume has been added to celebrate the SDV 1.5 update! 
- Set "BeachsideLifeguard" as "true" for his beach costume and portraits in the config.json.
*If "AnimePortraits" is set as "true", the Anime portrait will override the beach portrait.

Update 1.9 - Airyn made alternate sprites and portraits that match a cuter high-res version of Lucikiel.
- Set "AirynSpritesAndPortraits" as "true" in the config.json for their version.
*Overlaps with the Lifeguard costume. 
*If the "AnimePortraits" is set as "true", the Anime portrait will override Airyn's portrait.

Heart Events Guide:
- 0 Heart: Talk to Lucikiel once and enter town from 6:00 am - 5:30 pm, must be sunny
- 2 Heart: Enter Forest from 6:00 am - 7:00 pm, must be sunny, must be on Monday-Thurday or Saturday.
- 4 Heart: Enter Blacksmith from 9:00 am - 4:00 pm
- 6 Heart: Enter Mine from 9:00 am - 6:00 pm
- 8 Heart: Enter Beach from 8:00 pm - 11:50 pm, must not be raining

- 10 Heart:
**The final chapter. This time, your choices matter and determine the outcome of the story. There are two endings - true good ending, and the bad ending.
- You must have seen the 8 Heart event to trigger the 10 Heart events.
	Part One:
		- Upon reaching the 10 Heart requirements, Clint will visit the farm house and ask you to go to the forest at night. He suggests you take off any glow rings. (If you don't have any, then perfect.)
	Part Two:
		- Enter the Forest from 8:00 pm - 11:50 pm, must not be raining. 
		- You will see a Junimo trying to stop Lucikiel from entering the Woods. You will need an upgraded steel axe to remove the fallen log to follow him.
	Part Three:
		- Enter the Woods from 8:00 pm - 11:50 pm, must not be raining.
		- Choose wisely and follow your heart. Important decisions.
*The two endings have branching paths with different consequences. You will be notified if you run into the bad ending.

- 14 Heart: 
	Part One:
	    - Must have at least upgraded the house a second time.
	    - Enter the Farm House with Lucikiel present from 12:00 pm - 11:00 pm
	Part Two:
	    - Enter the beach from 6:10 AM - 12:00 PM, must be sunny
	    - Ultimate ending to Luckiel's storyline.

MARRIAGE SCHEDULES:
Lucikiel has three work days during the week. He leaves the farm house on Mondays (work), Fridays (work & hang-outs), and Saturdays (work-out at the gym) || NOTE: Interfering with time using the CJB Cheats Menu Mod will occasionally stop Lucikiel from continuing his marriage-work schedule.
